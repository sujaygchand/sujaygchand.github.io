DRIV-AR

>DRIV//AR is an Augmented reality service designed for particularly privateer car dealerships. DRIV//AR allows these businesses to communicate the auto industry to its customers more effectively. DRIV//AR is able to be successfully implemented into any business that sell cars, and can easily justify the interests of company management, manufacturers, and potential customers.

This project was presented on a Hololens and developed on Unity 3D.


THIS NOT MY PROJECT, rather I outsourced my skills to develop the functionality of the product. I programmed the fundamentals of the customiser and handled most of the backend. Where the designer could just rely on dropdown menus and public variables, when implementing the script components on the buttons and car parts.
