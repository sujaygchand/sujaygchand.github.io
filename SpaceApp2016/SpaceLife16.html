NZ SpaceApps 2016

This project was made in less than 24 hours for the NASA SpaceApps Challenge 2016. We managed to win the People Choice Award in New Zealand.

For the challenge we developed a concept for a Space survival exploration game. That teach individuals about space colonisation.
