HOMEJAM: HOMELESS EDITION

Submission for Global Game Jam 2019

A puzzle game about moving the cluttered mess (furniture and other objects) around the room in order to escape your house. The house is full of everyday objects and is presented in a flat shaded style.


Controls


W/A/S/D - Movement

Left Mouse Click - Grab/Release Object
