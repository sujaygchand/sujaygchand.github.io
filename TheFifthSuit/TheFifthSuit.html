THE FIFTH SUIT

This project was my groups final project in my second year of university, made in Unreal Engine 4. Initially, we aimed to create a parkour based game based on a alternative Alice in Wonderland; with a darker tone that explored human trafficking.

Nevertheless, we were a small team of 4 that attempted to do this project in less than 12 weeks. Many issues arose, thus we had to scrap together what we had and make a tech demo of the basic mechanics in one level.
