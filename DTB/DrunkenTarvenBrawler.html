DRUNKEN TAVERN BRAWLER

A multiplayer 3D Arena Brawler, where all the characters are drunk

This is my graduation project, a 3D fighter where I play with ragdoll and bone physics to create a drunken effect.

The aim of the game is to incapacitate your opponents and throw them out of the Tavern. The last player standing wins.

Group Roles:

Sujay Chand (Myself) - Programmer (Blueprint), Technical Artist, Game Designer and Project Manager

Ryan Sue - 3D Artist and Level Designer

Harry O'Connor - UI and Sound

Special thanks to:

William Singh for Music and a couple animations

Aldo Visini for lighting help

The Characters and majority of the animations were bought

When concepting this project, I was inspired by games like Shrek SuperSlam, Gang Beasts, Shrek SuperSlam, Super Smash Bros, Ultra Digimon All-Star Punchtime Battle Royale, Shrek SuperSlam and last but not least Solitaire.
