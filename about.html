About Me

I am a New Zealand born programmer and designer with a Bachelor's in Creative Technologies from Auckland University of Technology. Currently, I work as a contract Unreal Developer at Kara Technologies . I am always learning more to expand my skillset and enjoy experimenting with code, game engines and technical art.

Outside of Technology, I am a Martials Arts enthusiast and actively train Muay Thai. My training training philosophy is to not to go all out every day, rather I work towards having a highly productive week and ensuring that I am not too physically or mentally fatigued for the next day.
