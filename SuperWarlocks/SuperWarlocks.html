SUPER WARLOCKS

Kiwi-Jam 2016 submission.

This project was inspired by the Warlock mod from Warcraft 3. It was made in a 48 hour Game jam, on Unreal Engine 4. I was the lead programmer in this project.


The goal of the game to trying and blend in with AI and other players to shoot of the platform, with fireballs. First player to 5 kills, wins. But, be careful; the moment you shoot your position is given away.

You need a controller to play this game

CONTROLS:


Movement - Left Thumbstick

Shoot Fireball - Left/Right Trigger

Sprint - Left/Right Bumper

Skills I Used:

  • Unreal Engine 4
  • Game Design
